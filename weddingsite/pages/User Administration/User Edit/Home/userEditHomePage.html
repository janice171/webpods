Welcome to your admin area

This is the admin area for creating your website. We've produced some tutorials to get you started. If you can't wait to read the tutorials then click on the web pages links to the left and start editing your pages.

You can also look at doing the following...

  • Choose Your Website Theme:
    Go to your settings page and click on the 'Change Theme' link.
  • Add Your Events:
    Go to your events page and start adding all your wedding events. Once you've added your invites you can add your guests.
  • Send Invites:
    Once you've added your events and assigned your guests to these events you can start sending invites from the invites page.
